A Reputable Home Decor Store In Ludlow

Established in 1982, Ludlow Homecare Ltd has grown into a one-stop shop for all your house and garden needs, from a handful of screws to a fully designed new fitted kitchen. We can provide you with plants, trees, shrubs, seeds, pots, composts, treatments, fencing, timber, hoses, lawnmowers, blowers, hedge trimmers and everything else for your garden. Our experienced team of friendly staff can help you find what you need.

In addition, we also offer free delivery on all orders over £50

We have an extensive range of carpets with a free design service together with a selection of rugs, laminates, vinyl and engineered wood flooring. We have beds and furniture of all shapes and sizes for every room in your house, and a great selection of mirrors and lighting.

We will design and plan all your kitchen and bathroom requirements, from selecting a tap to providing a fully computerised visual image of how your new kitchen or bathroom will look.

We offer Free Delivery for all our kitchens, bathrooms and built-in appliances with orders over £50. Get in touch with us now to find out more about our products.
